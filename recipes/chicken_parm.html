Pan-Fried Chicken Parmesean

Pan-Fried Chicken Parmesean. Serves 4, Cook Time ~30 min

Found this recipe on budgetbytes. It is very moist, crispy, cheesy, and delicious.

No oven is required! It's easier to clean up and I think it tastes better.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesean cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • garlic powder
  • italian seasoning
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce

Steps

  1. filet each breast to make 4 cutlets
  2. season the breasts with salt, pepper, italian seasoning, and garlic powder
  3. in a wide bowl, combine the bread crumbs, permesean, garlic powder, italian seasoning, salt, and pepper
  4. in a seperate bowl, wisk the eggs
  5. in a seperate bowl, add the flour
  6. take each breast and dip it into the flour, then the eggs, and then the bread crumbs
  7. heat cooking oil in a large skillet over medium=high heat, cook the chicken until one side is golden brown (about 5 min)
  8. flip the chicken and turn the heat down a little. Add marinara sauce and mozzarella on top of each breast. Place a lid on the skillet to help heat up the sauce and cheese.
  9. finish cooking the chicken all the way through (another 5-7 min). Serve with spaghetti and more marinara!
