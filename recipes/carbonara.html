Lenguine Carbonara

Creamy Lenguine Carbonara. Serves 3-4, Cook Time ~1 hr

I always loved my dads sphaggheti carbonara, but over the years I have made modifications that suit my taste. My pasta uses thicker lenguine noodles and a cream sauce rather than an olive oil sauce. This is a quick, easy, comfy dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs bacon or prosciutto
  • 1 large onion diced
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 grated parmesean cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 16 oz linguine pasta
  • 4 cloves of garlic crushed

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce. Seperate the egg yolks from the whites, in a bowl mix the cream, cheese, and egg yolks together.
  2. Begin cooking the lenguine
  3. While the lenguine cooks, work on the bacon. Cut the bacon into small pieces and cook it in a large cast iron skillet
  4. Once the bacon is cooked, scoop it out and use a paper towel to damp out some of the grease.
  5. Sauttee the onions in the skillet with the remaining grease, throw in the garlic too. Add the bacon back in.
  6. Once the noodles are done, strain them and add them to the skillet.
  7. Pour in the cream sauce and stir well, also add in salt and pepper. Optionally top with parsely and more parmesean. Enjoy!
